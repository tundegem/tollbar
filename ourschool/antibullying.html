Toll Bar Primary pupils took part in Anti- Bullying Week

They took part in a range of activities in class and around school including:

  • Cyber bullying
  • Miss Dorothy.com
  • Friendship Books
  • Anti-Bullying writing

Pupils received anti-bullying pencils and bands.

The children all learnt the importance of TELL!

Toll Bar Primary is a NO Bully Zone.
