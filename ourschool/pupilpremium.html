Toll Bar Primary School has allocated pupil premiums funds for 2010/2011, 2011/2012 and 2012/ 2013 to a wide range of interventions to support the learning of the pupils. These include:

  • 1:1 Tuition in Literacy and Numeracy
  • Small group support across a wide range of subjects
  • Booster groups
  • Mentoring
  • 24/7 revision via mathletics and spellodrome
  • Study support
  • After-School clubs
  • Achievement awards and prizes
  • Enrichment activities e.g. Arts weeks
  • Small group support for FSM pupils and other vulnerable pupils who are underachieving

Each year we will review the impact of the measures listed to ensure they represent both value for money and played a direct role in improving the achievement of pupils of all abilities.
