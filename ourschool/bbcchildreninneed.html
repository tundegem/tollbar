In 2014 the pupils and staff at Toll Bar Primary School have today raised £650 for BBC Children in Need. Everyone has really enjoyed the many activities, from sponsored singing, drama, raffle, buns sale and even a sponsored leg wax!

Toll Bar Primary Pupils took part in Children in Need - the pupils and staff raised £218.90 in 2009, £234.11 in 2010, £329.34 in 2011 and £198.17 in 2012 through a range of activitites:

  • Non-uniform or pyjamas day
  • Pudsey buns and biscuits on sale
  • Raffle
  • A giant Pudsey was covered in 2p and 1p's
  • To end the day we had fun in the hall!
